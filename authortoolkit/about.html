First of all – welcome!
We are SNØ, a new imprint of Birch Tree Publishing, and publish titles across fiction and non-fiction. Our mission is to find brilliant, fresh voices across the storytelling world and promote our new authors as unique and special.
In the past we have published titles affiliated with the Scandinavian lifestyle, but now wish to expand our fictional and non-fictional repertoire by giving the readers stories which intrigue and fascinate, where you are left with a sense of well-being.
If you think you are the new, aspiring author we are looking for and that you can recognise your style of writing with our booklist, please contact us (more info on the Contact page). We take our partnerships with authors seriously and will always strive to put you as an individual at our core.
